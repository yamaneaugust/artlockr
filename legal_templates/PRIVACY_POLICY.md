## 3. Data Storage and Retention

### 3.1 Privacy-First Storage

**Default Mode: Features-Only**

When you upload artwork:

1. Image is temporarily loaded into memory
2. Feature vector (2048 dimensions) is extracted
3. **Original image is DELETED immediately**
4. Only feature vector is stored in database
5. Cryptographic hash proves ownership

**Result:** Your artwork is NEVER stored on our servers (by default).

### 3.2 Storage Options

You can choose:

| Mode | Original Image | Features | Privacy | Use Case |
|------|----------------|----------|---------|----------|
| **Features-Only** | ❌ Deleted immediately | ✅ Stored | Maximum | Default |
| **Temporary** | ⏱️ Deleted after 24 hours | ✅ Stored | High | Multi-metric detection |
| **Permanent** | ✅ Stored | ✅ Stored | Moderate | Full detection capabilities |

### 3.3 Data Retention Periods

| Data Type | Retention Period | Rationale |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------|
| Feature vectors | Until deletion | Core service functionality |
| Account info | Until account deletion | Account management |
| Detection results | 2 years or until deletion | Historical tracking |
| Audit logs | 7 years | Security and compliance |
| Payment records | 7 years | Tax and legal requirements |
| Cookies | Session to 1 year | Session management and preferences |

### 3.4 Data Deletion

When you delete:

- **Artwork:** Feature vector deleted permanently
- **Account:** All data deleted within 30 days
- **Detection results:** Deleted immediately

**Secure Deletion:**
- Files are overwritten before deletion
- Database records are permanently removed
- Backups are purged within 90 days

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### 5.1 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have:

**Right to Access:**
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Receive data in a portable format (JSON/CSV)

**Right to Rectification:**
- Correct inaccurate data
- Update incomplete data

**Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):**
- Request deletion of your data
- Permanent deletion within 30 days

**Right to Restriction:**
- Limit how we process your data
- Object to certain processing activities

**Right to Data Portability:**
- Receive your data in machine-readable format
- Transfer data to another service

**Right to Object:**
- Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- Object to marketing communications (opt-out)

**Right to Withdraw Consent:**
- Withdraw consent for optional data processing
- Does not affect prior lawful processing

**Right to Complain:**
- Lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority

### 5.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have:

**Right to Know:**
- Categories of personal information collected
- Sources of information
- Purposes for collection
- Third parties with whom we share data

**Right to Delete:**
- Request deletion of personal information
- Exceptions for legal obligations

**Right to Opt-Out:**
- Opt-out of sale of personal information
- (We don't sell data, so this is automatic)

**Right to Non-Discrimination:**
- Equal service regardless of privacy choices
- No penalties for exercising rights

### 5.3 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise your rights:

1. **Email:** privacy@artlockr.com
2. **Account Settings:** Use privacy dashboard
3. **API Endpoints:** `/api/privacy/my-data`, `/api/privacy/delete-all`

**Verification:**
- We may request verification to prevent unauthorized access
- Respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA)
